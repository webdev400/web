How It Works

One app. Two powerful interfaces.

Zapienz is designed from the ground up with two separate experiences — one built for children, one built for the adults who care for them.

Child Mode

A Safe AI Companion

Children explore, ask questions, and learn through natural conversation. Every response is age-appropriate, educational, and safe.

Parent Mode

Full Control & Visibility

Monitor activity, set content filters, configure alerts, and receive real-time notifications — all from a private, PIN-protected parent dashboard.
