# About Us — Zapienz by Socrates AI

---

## Company Overview

**Socrates AI** is an Argentine technology startup founded in 2026, headquartered in Buenos Aires, Argentina. We are the company behind **Zapienz** — the first AI platform designed specifically for children ages 5 to 12, with a focus on safe exploration, education, and robust parental control.

We stand at the intersection of education, technology, parenting, and ethics. While children are already using AI — whether anyone teaches them or not — parents don't know how to guide that process, schools are lagging behind, and governments are still catching up. Zapienz was built to fill that gap.

---

## Mission

Provide a safe and educational AI exploration tool for children ages 5 to 12. Facilitate learning and personal development through the use of artificial intelligence — without creating dependency on it.

---

## Vision

Make Zapienz the leading AI for children in Latin America and worldwide, delivering innovation, safety, and control in every interaction.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Children today face three compounding risks in the digital world:

- **Online risks:** Exposure to inappropriate content, lack of safe and age-appropriate AI tools.
- **Lack of control:** No AI platform currently gives parents meaningful oversight of what their children are doing and asking.
- **Lack of personalization:** Educational tools fail to adapt to each child's individual needs, limiting their development.

Meanwhile, parents are left without tools. Schools are behind. And no existing application alerts a parent if their child is experiencing bullying, grooming, or emotional distress through an AI interaction — until now.

---

## Our Solution: Zapienz

Zapienz is an artificial intelligence designed specifically for children ages 5 to 12. It offers safe and controlled access to information, with a focus on exploration, learning, education, and personal development — all through a dual interface and under robust parental controls.

### For Children — A Safe Companion
- Age-appropriate AI conversations designed for curiosity and learning
- Content adapted to each child's age, interests, and development stage
- A warm, engaging interface that makes AI exploration fun and educational

### For Parents — Full Control and Peace of Mind
- **Parental control panel:** Configure topics, content filters, and conversation boundaries
- **Real-time alerts:** Immediate notifications when the AI detects sensitive or dangerous situations
- **Red Flags System:** Our proprietary AI-powered detection system identifies signs of bullying, grooming, self-harm, abuse, and emotional distress in conversations — and alerts parents instantly
- **Activity monitoring:** Full visibility into your child's AI interactions
- **Reports and summaries:** Regular digests of your child's activity

This is our core differentiator. No other application today notifies parents when their child is experiencing harassment, abuse, or dangerous emotional states through an AI interaction. **Zapienz does.**

## Technology

Zapienz is built on a multi-agent AI architecture:

- A **child interface** that delivers safe, educational, age-appropriate AI responses
- A **parental control system** that configures the ethical and content parameters of each conversation
- A **post-processing layer** that verifies parental controls and detects sensitive content in real time
- Specialized AI agents: educational, emotional containment, moral guidance, and more

The app is available on **Android** and **iOS**, developed with FlyDevs.

## Why "Cognitive Sovereignty"?

Our mission is not to teach children how to use AI. Our mission is to form children who **understand AI without depending on it.** We call this *cognitive sovereignty* — the ability to think critically, creatively, and independently in a world full of AI assistants.

Children who grow up with Zapienz will be:
- More critical thinkers
- More creative problem-solvers
- More autonomous learners
- More human

We are not building an app. We are designing how the next generation will coexist with artificial intelligence.
