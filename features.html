Product Features

Everything Zapienz
Can Do.

A complete platform built for children's safety, learning, and growth — and for the parents who guide them. Every feature is purposeful, every interaction is protected.

Core Features

Six pillars of a safer digital childhood.

Adapted Content

Age-appropriate AI exploration tailored for children ages 5–12. Every response is calibrated to the child's age, interests, and development stage — nothing more, nothing less.

Advanced Security

Next-generation parental controls — configurable content filters, topic boundaries, and a PIN-protected parent dashboard. Full control, always.

Dual Interface

Two entirely separate experiences in one app. Child mode for fun, safe exploration. Parent mode for monitoring, alerts, reports, and fine-grained filters.

Red Flags System

Our proprietary AI detection system identifies signs of bullying, grooming, self-harm, and abuse in conversations — and alerts parents instantly. No other platform does this.

Education

Personalized educational support based on each child's progress, interests, and learning style. High-quality content that makes learning feel natural and exciting.

Skills Development

Prepares children to understand and use AI critically — without dependence. We build cognitive sovereignty: the ability to think independently in a world full of AI.

Child Mode

A Safe AI Companion
built for curiosity.

The child interface is warm, engaging, and designed from the ground up for young minds. Every answer is age-appropriate and educational, designed to support safe exploration.

  • Natural conversation

    Children explore and ask questions through free-form chat — no complicated menus.

  • Age-calibrated responses

    Content adapts to the child's age group — different for a 5-year-old than a 12-year-old.

  • Educational reinforcement

    Specialised AI agents for education, emotional support, moral guidance, and more.

  • Available on Android & iOS

    Works on any smartphone or tablet — wherever children learn and play.

Parent Mode

Full Control &
Complete Visibility.

The parent dashboard gives you everything you need to feel confident about your child's AI experience — in real time, from your own PIN-protected space.

  • Parental control panel

    Configure topics, content filters, and conversation boundaries for each child.

  • Real-time alerts

    Instant push notifications when the AI detects sensitive or dangerous situations.

  • Activity monitoring

    Full visibility into your child's AI interactions — what they asked, what they received.

  • Reports & summaries

    Regular digests of your child's activity, topics explored, and learning milestones.

Under the Hood

Built on multi-agent AI architecture.

Every interaction flows through four specialised layers — each one ensuring safety, accuracy, and appropriateness before a child ever sees a response.

1

Child Interface Agent

Delivers safe, educational, age-appropriate AI responses in natural conversation.

2

Parental Control System

Configures the ethical and content parameters of each conversation based on parent settings.

3

Post-Processing Layer

Verifies parental controls and detects sensitive content in real time before delivery.

4

Specialised AI Agents

Dedicated agents for education, emotional containment, moral guidance, and Red Flags detection.
